# Privacy policy — MyArrows

*Last updated: June 23, 2026*

[Versión en español](privacy-policy.md)

**MyArrows** is an archery arrow calculator app developed by **GFF** (German Fuentes). This policy explains what information is processed when you use the Android app.

## 1. Data controller

- **GFF** — [german.fuentes@gmail.com](mailto:german.fuentes@gmail.com)
- **Application package:** `com.gff`

## 2. Data we process

### 2.1 Data stored only on your device

- **Language preference:** stored locally. Not sent to GFF servers.
- **Calculator inputs:** processed on-device using embedded SQLite. GFF does not receive or store this data on its servers.

### 2.2 Data processed by third parties (advertising)

The app displays **banner ads** via **Google AdMob** on Home, Menu and Settings (not on the calculator). Google may collect advertising ID, IP address, ad interactions and device/app information. See [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 3. App permissions

- **Internet:** required to load AdMob ads.

The app does not request location, contacts, camera or microphone.

## 4. Your rights & children

EEA users may contact [german.fuentes@gmail.com](mailto:german.fuentes@gmail.com) to exercise GDPR rights. The app is not directed at children under 13.
