Privacy policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

MyArrows is an archery arrow calculator app developed by GFF (German Fuentes). This policy explains what information is processed when you use the Android app.

1. Data controller

GFF — german.fuentes@gmail.com
Application package: com.gff

2. Data we process

2.1 Data stored only on your device

  • Language preference: stored locally. Not sent to GFF servers.
  • Calculator inputs: draw weight, length, bow type, manufacturer, FPS, etc. Processed on-device using an embedded SQLite database. GFF does not receive or store this data on its own servers.

2.2 Data processed by third parties (advertising)

The app displays banner ads via Google AdMob on Home, Menu and Settings (not on the calculator). Google may collect advertising ID, IP address, ad interactions and device/app information. See Google's privacy policy.

3. App permissions

  • Internet: required to load AdMob ads.

The app does not request location, contacts, camera or microphone.

4. Retention & your rights

Local data remains until you uninstall the app or clear its data. EEA users may contact german.fuentes@gmail.com to exercise GDPR rights.

5. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13.

6. Contact

german.fuentes@gmail.com
