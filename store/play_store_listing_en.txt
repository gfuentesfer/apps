Short description (max 80 chars):
Arrow spine calculator for recurve, barebow, traditional and compound bows.

Full description:
MyArrows helps you choose the right arrow spine and shaft models for your bow setup.

Features:
• Spine calculator based on draw weight, arrow length, bow type and shooting style
• Olympic recurve, barebow, traditional and compound bow support
• Easton, Victory and Skylon model catalog
• Compound bow catalog by brand and model
• "I don't know FPS" option when bow speed is unknown
• Spanish and English UI

Works offline: catalog and calculations on your device. No account required.
